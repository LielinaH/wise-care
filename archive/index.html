Prototype overview · v0.1

Wise Care — a calm path to mental health support.

Sixteen screens across user, provider, admin, and organization sides — designed as a complete digital product, not a chatbot or single intake form. Simulated data only. No diagnoses, no medication advice, no medical claims.
